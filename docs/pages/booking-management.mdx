# Booking Management Guide

The Booking Management section is where you handle all guest reservations for your campsite. This guide will help you efficiently manage bookings through their entire lifecycle.

## What You Can Do Here

- **View All Bookings**: See a complete list of all reservations
- **Filter and Search**: Find specific bookings quickly
- **Process New Bookings**: Confirm and manage incoming reservations
- **Modify Bookings**: Change dates, tent assignments, or guest details
- **Handle Check-ins/Check-outs**: Process guests arriving and departing
- **Cancel Bookings**: Process cancellations when necessary

## Using the Booking List

When you open the Booking Management page, you'll see a comprehensive list of all bookings. The table includes:

- **Booking ID**: Unique reference number for each reservation
- **Guest Name**: The primary guest who made the booking
- **Check-in/Check-out Dates**: When guests arrive and depart
- **Tent**: The assigned accommodation
- **Status**: Current state of the booking (Pending, Confirmed, Checked-in, etc.)
- **Payment Status**: Whether the booking has been paid
- **Actions**: Buttons to view details, edit, or process the booking

### Finding Specific Bookings

To locate particular bookings:

1. **Use the search box**: Enter booking ID, guest name, or phone number
2. **Apply filters**: Click the filter icon to narrow results by:
   - Date range
   - Booking status
   - Tent type
   - Payment status
3. **Sort columns**: Click column headers to arrange bookings by that information

## Managing Booking Status

### Processing New Bookings

When new reservations come in:

1. Find the booking in the "Pending" status
2. Click the **View Details** button to review all information
3. Verify availability and payment information
4. Click **Confirm** to approve the booking
5. The system will automatically send a confirmation to the guest

### Handling Check-ins

When guests arrive:

1. Search for their booking using name or booking ID
2. Verify their identity and booking details
3. Click the **Check-in** button
4. Record any additional information or special requests
5. Provide guests with necessary information about their stay

### Processing Check-outs

When guests are leaving:

1. Find their booking in the system
2. Check if there are any outstanding payments or charges
3. Click the **Check-out** button
4. Confirm the check-out process is complete
5. The tent will be marked as available for cleaning

### Managing Cancellations

If a guest needs to cancel:

1. Locate their booking
2. Click the **Cancel** button
3. Select the appropriate cancellation reason
4. Determine refund eligibility based on your cancellation policy
5. Confirm the cancellation
6. If a refund is due, you'll be prompted to process it via the Refund Management section

## Making Changes to a Booking

To modify an existing booking:

1. Find the booking in the list
2. Click the **Edit** button
3. Make necessary changes to:
   - Dates
   - Tent assignment
   - Guest information
   - Special requests
4. Click **Save** to apply changes
5. The system will notify the guest about significant changes
