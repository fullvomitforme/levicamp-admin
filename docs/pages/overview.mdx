# Dashboard Overview

<Callout type="info">
  The Dashboard Overview is your daily command center for monitoring the campsite's performance and operations.
</Callout>

## What You Can See

### Key Metrics

<Cards>
  <Card title="Revenue" href="#">
    Total earnings with percentage change from last month
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bookings" href="#">
    Total number of reservations with month-over-month comparison
  </Card>
  <Card title="Active Tents" href="#">
    Current occupancy status showing active vs. total tents
  </Card>
  <Card title="Growth Rate" href="#">
    Business growth indicators compared to last month
  </Card>
</Cards>

### Revenue Charts

<Tabs items={["Revenue Overview", "Revenue Breakdown"]}>
  <Tab>
    This chart shows your earnings trend over time, helping you identify patterns and predict future performance.
    
    <Callout>
      Hover over data points to see exact values for specific dates.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>
  <Tab>
    This pie chart displays how your revenue is distributed across different categories, such as tent types or additional services.
    
    <Callout>
      Click on segments to see detailed breakdowns for each category.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Guest Statistics

<Callout>
  The Total Guest Chart shows occupancy trends over time, helping you identify peak seasons and plan staffing accordingly.
</Callout>

### Recent Bookings

A list of the most recent reservations, allowing you to:

  </Card>
  <Card title="New Bookings" href="#">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Booking Details" href="#">
    Quickly access comprehensive booking information
  </Card>
</Cards>

## Using the Dashboard

<Tabs items={["Getting Started", "Reading Data", "Taking Action"]}>
  <Tab>
    ### When You First Log In
    
    <Steps>
      <Step>
        Wait for all metrics and charts to load completely
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Review key metrics at the top of the dashboard
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Examine revenue charts and guest statistics
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Check recent bookings for upcoming arrivals
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab>
    ### Interpreting Trend Indicators
    
    <Cards>
      <Card title="Positive Trends" href="#">
        Upward arrows (green) indicate positive growth or improvement
      </Card>
      <Card title="Negative Trends" href="#">
        Downward arrows (red) indicate negative trends requiring attention
      </Card>
    </Cards>
    
    <Callout type="warning">
      The dashboard refreshes automatically, but you can manually update it by refreshing your browser if needed.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>
  <Tab>
    ### Taking Action
    
    From the dashboard, you can:
    
    <Steps>
      <Step>
        Click on any booking in the Recent Bookings list to view its details
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Navigate to specific sections using the sidebar menu
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Export reports for meetings or record-keeping using the export buttons
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Best Practices

<Callout type="info">
  Follow these tips to get the most out of your dashboard experience:
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Check daily**: Review the dashboard at the beginning of your shift to understand current status
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Monitor trends**: Focus on patterns over time rather than single-day fluctuations
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Use insights strategically**: Apply revenue breakdown data to inform marketing and pricing strategies
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Follow up on alerts**: Investigate any metrics showing negative trends promptly
  </Step>
</Steps>
