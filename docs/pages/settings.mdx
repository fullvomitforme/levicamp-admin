# Settings

<Callout type="info">
  The Settings page allows you to manage your personal account details, security preferences, and system configurations.
</Callout>

## Account Management

### Personal Information

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Navigate to the Settings page from the sidebar menu
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review your current profile information in the Personal Details section
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click the Edit button to update your information
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Update your name, email, or phone number as needed
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click Save to apply your changes
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
  Updating your email address may require verification through a confirmation link sent to the new email.
</Callout>

### Changing Your Password

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click the "Change Password" button in the security section
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Enter your current password for verification
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Enter and confirm your new password
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Follow password strength guidelines for security (minimum 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click Submit to save your new password
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="error">
  You will be immediately logged out after changing your password and will need to log in using your new credentials.
</Callout>

## Notification Preferences

Control how and when you receive system notifications:

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Navigate to the Notifications section of the Settings page
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Enable or disable different notification types:
    - New bookings
    - Check-in reminders
    - Check-out alerts
    - Refund requests
    - System updates
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Select your preferred notification methods:
    - Email notifications
    - Browser notifications
    - Mobile push notifications (if mobile app is installed)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Set quiet hours when you don't want to receive non-urgent notifications
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Save your preferences
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Advanced Settings

<Callout type="warning">
  Changes to advanced settings may affect system behavior. Only make changes if you understand their impact.
</Callout>

### Data Export

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click the "Export My Data" button in the Advanced section
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Select the data categories you want to export
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Choose your preferred export format (CSV, JSON, or PDF)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click Generate Export to create your data file
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Download the file when ready
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Account Deactivation

<Callout type="error">
  Account deactivation is different from deletion. Deactivation temporarily suspends your access while preserving your account data.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Scroll to the Account Status section
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click "Deactivate Account"
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Select your reason for deactivation from the dropdown menu
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Enter your password to confirm
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click Confirm Deactivation
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<Tabs items={["Password Issues", "Email Verification", "Display Problems"]}>
  <Tab>
    ### Password Issues
    
    <Steps>
      <Step>
        If you forgot your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page
      </Step>
      <Step>
        If you're getting "Incorrect Password" errors when you're sure it's correct, try clearing your browser cache
      </Step>
      <Step>
        For persistent issues, contact your system administrator
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab>
    ### Email Verification
    
    <Steps>
      <Step>
        Check your spam/junk folder for verification emails
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Use the "Resend Verification" option if you didn't receive the email
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Ensure you're checking the correct email account
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab>
    ### Display Problems
    
    <Steps>
      <Step>
        Try refreshing your browser if settings changes don't appear
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Clear your browser cache if display issues persist
      </Step>
      <Step>
        Test in a different browser to identify browser-specific issues
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
